Wind Speed

This plot is showing the distribution of the wind speeds for a city based on the latitude the city lays on. The speeds are generally clustered below 15mph, but there are some extreme speeds the further the city is from the equator (0 lat). Other then this, there seems to be no correlation between wind speeds and distance from the equatior.
